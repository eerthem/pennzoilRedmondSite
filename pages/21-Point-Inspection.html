21 Point Inspection

Ever wondered what exactly our 21 point inspection consists of? 

  1. Replace Oil up to 5 Quarts
  2. Replace Oil Filter
  3. Replace Drain Plug Gasket
  4. Inspect Air Filter
  5. Check Coolant
  6. Check Transmission Fluid
  7. Check Belts
  8. Check Hoses
  9. Chick Tire Pressure
  10. Check Battery
  11. Check & Fill Washer Fluid
  12. Check Wiper Blades
  13. Check all Exterior Lights
  14. Check Fuel Filter
  15. Check Cabin Air Filter
  16. Check Power Steering
  17. Check Brake Fluid
  18. Check Clutch Fluid
  19. Courtesy Windshield Wash
  20. Courtesy Vacuum**
  21. Reset Maintenance Light
